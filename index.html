Welcome to SpeedCube Hub! This website introduces the exciting hobby of speedcubing. Speedcubing is the sport of solving puzzles like the Rubik's Cube as fast as possible.

About Speedcubing

The Rubik’s Cube was invented in 1974 by Ernő Rubik. Today millions of people around the world compete to solve it as fast as possible.

  • Improves memory
  • Improves problem solving
  • Fun hobby
  • Competitive sport
